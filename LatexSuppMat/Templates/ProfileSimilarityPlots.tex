Boxplots depict the distribution of profile similarities for designable positions in each protein-ligand complex in the
binding site recovery benchmark for varying speical\_rot weights. Designable positions that achieved high profile
similarity (>0.9) regardless of modifications to the Packer were removed from these plots. Aggregate shows statistics
for all complexes in the benchmark set.
